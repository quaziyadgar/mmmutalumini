About Us

MMMUT University is the oldest college in Uttar Pradesh and became a university in 2013.It is located in Gorakhpur, deoria road (UP). The present campus has a sprawling 354 acre area in the heart of Gorakhpur. This university is recently awarded with 'A' grade by NAAC and became the first top 1 university in this state.

Vision
“To bring together the entire alumni and help our university become a world class institution."
Mission
To develop and strengthen ties between the alumni of MMMUT University and the university through well-managed initiatives in diverse areas such as:
  • Career services and networking opportunities.
  • Scholarships and financial aid for deserving students.
  • Public lectures and events through physical and digital media.
  • Awards for meritorious students as well as distinguished alumni and faculty members.
  • Mobilizing mentoring and funding support to students and alumni taking entrepreneurial path.
  • Partnering with the university administration for strengthening MMMUT University’s institutional governance and eclectic development.
